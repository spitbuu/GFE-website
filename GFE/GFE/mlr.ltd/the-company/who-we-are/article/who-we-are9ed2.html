Who we are

As a true "glocal" player, we encourage and promote business growth by integrating local and global dynamics.

MLR is a consulting company based in the Indochinese peninsula, with offices in Vietnam, Thailand, and Myanmar. We guide our clients toward business success by offering attentive listening, a medium- and long-term strategic vision, and an in-depth analysis of internal and external factors.

Our ambition is to support local businesses in their international development while fostering exchanges between the local economic fabric and foreign companies wishing to invest in the region. We strive to minimize risks and negative impacts associated with any investment project.

This region benefits from a strategic geographic position in the Indo-Pacific zone and remarkable economic dynamism, offering one of the most significant potential for business creation in Southeast Asia. With our strong regional presence and extensive network, we are committed to contributing to the growth of businesses and their sustainable success in this promising region.

MLR stands out for its local expertise and global perspective, enabling us to provide tailored and innovative solutions for every project. We are convinced that our in-depth knowledge of the field and our ability to anticipate market trends make us a preferred partner for any company seeking to thrive in Indochina.
