Risk advisory

In the course of our Risk Advisory missions, we support our clients by bringing various risks to light in order for them to adopt the right strategy and course of actions.

Background check

We offer comprehensive and rigorous compliance and background check services that are essential for assessing the solidity and transparency of a company before any strategic or transactional decision.

Due Diligence

Regulatory due diligence (RDD) is an evaluation conducted by a company to assess the level of regulatory compliance of the target company in the context of an acquisition or merger.

Financial Due Diligence

Financial due diligence is an essential process when evaluating a company, particularly in the context of an acquisition, merger, or major investment. It primarily involves an in-depth accounting audit to examine the financial health of the target company.
