The Foreign Trade Advisors of France

4500 men and women leaders and business executives, international experts serving France’s development for over 120 years. They are present in all regions of France and in over 150 countries.

Business France

Business France is an organization born from the merger of Ubifrance and the French Agency for International Investments (AFII). Created by Ordinance No. 2014-1555 on December 22, 2014, Business France has been operational since January 1, 2015.

The French Chamber of Commerce and Industry in Vietnam (CCIFV)

The French Chamber of Commerce and Industry France-Vietnam (CCIFV), founded in 1989 at the initiative of business leaders in Ho Chi Minh City, is a local non-profit association. It is part of the global network of France International Chambers of Commerce and Industry (CCI France International), which brings together 119 chambers in 94 countries.

French Tech Vietnam

The French Tech Mission is an administration attached to the General Directorate of Enterprises, within the Ministry of Economy, Finance, and Industrial and Digital Sovereignty. Its mission is to support the development of the French Tech ecosystem, a French movement of innovative tech startups, in line with governmental priorities, representing and serving French citizens.

Stratexio

Stratexio is an international growth accelerator founded on experience sharing and expertise acquisition. Created by employers’ organizations, Stratexio aims to stimulate the growth of French companies in international markets.
