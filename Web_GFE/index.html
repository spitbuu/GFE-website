GPF

A federation of business organizations representing over 3 million companies across the francophone world.

GPF International Network

Founded in 1987, the GPF (GPF) is a federation of business organizations representing a network of over 3 million companies across the francophone world.

GPF operates as a platform for economic cooperation, facilitating connections between francophone businesses, promoting trade exchanges, and supporting the sharing of best practices across regions. It brings together institutions, companies, and stakeholders across Europe, Africa, and Asia, contributing to the development of a globally connected francophone economic ecosystem.

GPF focuses on strengthening economic and institutional collaboration through four key areas: supporting commercial initiatives and business connections, promoting investment and economic development across francophone countries, providing access to economic intelligence and institutional networks, and connecting organizations through the shared language and culture of the francophone world.

GFE's Role

Through its leadership, GFE is engaged within the GPF network, contributing to initiatives that strengthen connections between Vietnam and the francophone world — supporting partnerships between institutions, universities, and organizations, while facilitating dialogue and collaboration across economic, educational, and cultural sectors.

Ho Chi Minh City Peace & Development Foundation

A non-profit foundation promoting inclusive development, cultural identity, and international cooperation.

HPDF

HPDF is a non-profit organization established by the Ho Chi Minh City People's Committee and operating within the framework of the Ho Chi Minh City Union of Friendship Organizations.

HPDF is part of a broader tradition of peace and development initiatives in Vietnam, inspired by the Vietnam Peace and Development Foundation, founded in 2003 by Mme. Nguyen Thi Binh, former Vice President of Vietnam. HPDF is now led by Mme. Ton Nu Thi Ninh, a prominent diplomat and advocate for international cooperation, whose leadership has shaped the Foundation's vision of dialogue, inclusivity, and global engagement.

The Foundation promotes peace, friendship, cooperation, and sustainable development, while strengthening Vietnam's engagement with international partners and communities. Building on this foundation, HPDF focuses on fostering inclusive development, cultural identity, and dialogue across local and global contexts — promoting education, community engagement, and sustainable societal development while connecting a broad network of professionals, institutions, and international partners.

GFE's Role

GFE is represented at the Board of Trustees of HPDF, with its founder among the youngest members invited to contribute to the Foundation's governance. In this capacity, GFE contributes to initiatives that support education, foster community development, and promote Vietnamese cultural identity, while engaging with a diverse network of local and international stakeholders.

Viet Nam Peace Committee

One of Vietnam's earliest organizations in people-to-people diplomacy and international peace advocacy.

Viet Nam Peace Committee

Uỷ ban hoà bình Việt Nam

The Viet Nam Peace Committee (VPC) is one of Vietnam's earliest and most prominent organizations in the field of people-to-people diplomacy and international peace advocacy.

Established on 19 November 1950 under the initiative of President Ho Chi Minh, the Committee was founded as part of Vietnam's engagement with the global peace movement and international solidarity efforts. Vietnam was among the founding participants of the World Peace Council, reinforcing the country's early role in global peace dialogue.

Operating under the Viet Nam Union of Friendship Organizations, the Committee continues to play a key role in connecting Vietnam with international partners and advancing cooperation through dialogue and exchange.

The Committee works to promote peace, cooperation, and mutual understanding across nations, while strengthening people-to-people diplomacy as a key channel of international engagement. Its activities contribute to connecting Vietnam with global peace initiatives, fostering collaboration with international organizations, and supporting sustainable development through dialogue-driven approaches.

GFE's Role

GFE is aligned with institutions such as the Viet Nam Peace Committee through shared commitments to peace, international cooperation, and sustainable development. Through this alignment, GFE engages in initiatives that support dialogue, knowledge exchange, and collaboration between Vietnam and international partners, particularly in areas related to education, community development, and cross-cultural engagement.
