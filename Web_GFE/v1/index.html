Who We Are

A glocal advisory firm deeply rooted in Southeast Asia's business landscape.

A True Glocal Player

Founded on the principle that local expertise and global standards are not mutually exclusive, GFE Company Limited serves as a bridge between international corporations and the complex business environments of Vietnam, Thailand, and Myanmar.

We combine on-the-ground intelligence with international best practices to deliver insights and strategies that are both actionable and culturally informed. Our team includes local experts, compliance specialists, and industry veterans who understand the nuances of Southeast Asian markets.

With a network spanning government institutions, business chambers, and private enterprises across the region, GFE is uniquely positioned to accelerate your business success.
